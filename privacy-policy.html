Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2025

Overview

Judging You is a personal habit and daily life tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

The short version: Your data is yours. We don't sell it, we don't use it for advertising, and we don't share it with third parties.

Data We Collect

Anonymous Mode

If you use the app without signing in:

  • All your data is stored locally on your device only
  • We do not collect or transmit any personal information
  • We have no access to your tracking data
  • If you uninstall the app, your data is permanently deleted

Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address – for account identification
  • Display name and profile photo – from your Google account
  • Your tracking data – categories, daily scores, and entries you create

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core functionality
  • To sync your data across devices (Google sign-in users only)
  • To restore your data if you reinstall the app

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Analyze your data for any purpose other than providing the service

Data Storage & Security

Anonymous Mode

Data is stored only on your device using secure local storage. It is not backed up to any cloud service.

Google Sign-In

Data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with the following security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firestore security rules ensure only you can access your data
  • Your data is isolated from other users

Data Deletion

Anonymous Mode

Simply uninstall the app or clear app data from your device settings. All data will be permanently deleted.

Google Sign-In

To delete your data:

  • Contact us at judgingyoutheapp@gmail.com
  • We will delete all your data from our servers within 30 days

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – for Google sign-in functionality
  • Cloud Firestore – for secure data storage (signed-in users only)
  • Google Play Billing – for optional in-app purchases

These services are provided by Google and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Judging You is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us
